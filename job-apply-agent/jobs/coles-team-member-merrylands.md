## About the role

Coles is looking for friendly, reliable casual Team Members for our Merrylands store.
You'll rotate across checkouts, the service desk, and the shop floor — helping
customers, keeping shelves stocked, and supporting the team during our busiest times.

## Responsibilities

- Serve customers at the checkout/self-serve area and assist with bagging and queries
- Help at the service desk with returns and general customer questions
- Stock and rotate shelves, follow date-checking and presentation standards
- Keep work areas clean, tidy and safe in line with store procedures
- Pitch in wherever the team needs support during peak trading periods

## Requirements

- A genuine, friendly approach to customer service
- Comfortable working as part of a team in a fast-paced retail environment
- Reliable and punctual — able to commit to a regular casual roster
- Available across a mix of weekday, evening, weekend and public holiday shifts
- Basic numeracy for handling registers and cash

## Nice to have

- Experience speaking community languages (Merrylands has a very diverse customer base)
- A current driver's licence and own transport
- Willingness to obtain a Responsible Service of Alcohol (RSA) certificate
