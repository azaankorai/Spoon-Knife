## About the role

JB Hi-Fi Parramatta is looking for enthusiastic casual Sales Assistants to help
customers find the right tech, games, and entertainment products, and to keep the
store running smoothly on the floor and at the registers.

## Responsibilities

- Greet customers and help them find products that suit their needs
- Operate the POS/register and process sales, exchanges and returns
- Keep shelves, displays and demo areas stocked, tidy and on-brand
- Stay up to date on current products and promotions to better assist customers
- Support the team during busy trading periods (weekends, sales events, holidays)

## Requirements

- A friendly, confident manner and genuine interest in helping people
- Comfortable learning POS/register systems and product information
- Reliable, punctual, and a good team player in a fast-paced environment
- Available for flexible shifts, including evenings, weekends and public holidays
- Comfortable with light stock handling (unpacking, shelving, moving displays)

## Nice to have

- An interest in tech, gaming, music or entertainment products
- Previous retail or customer service experience (training provided either way)
- Ability to communicate with customers from diverse language backgrounds
