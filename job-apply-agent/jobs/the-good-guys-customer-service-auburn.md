## About the role

The Good Guys Auburn is looking for casual Customer Service Team Members to support
our sales floor — helping customers at the registers and service counter, assisting
with deliveries and click-and-collect, and keeping the store running smoothly.

## Responsibilities

- Greet customers and direct them to the right department or team member
- Operate the POS/register, process payments, and handle simple returns/exchanges
- Assist with click-and-collect orders, stock movements and store presentation
- Answer general customer questions and escalate technical queries to specialists
- Support the team during busy trading periods (weekends, sales events, holidays)

## Requirements

- A friendly, helpful manner and willingness to learn about the products we sell
- Comfortable learning POS/register and order-management systems (training provided)
- Reliable, punctual, and a good communicator who works well in a team
- Available for flexible shifts — including evenings, weekends and public holidays —
  around 4 days a week
- Comfortable with light lifting and moving stock/boxes around the store

## Nice to have

- An interest in technology, appliances or electronics
- Previous customer service or retail experience (not essential — full training given)
- A driver's licence and own transport, and/or other languages spoken
