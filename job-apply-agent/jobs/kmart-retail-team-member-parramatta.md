## About the role

Kmart Parramatta is on the lookout for casual Retail Team Members to help deliver a
great in-store experience for our customers — from the moment they walk in to the
moment they check out.

## Responsibilities

- Provide friendly, helpful service on the shop floor and at registers
- Process transactions, returns and exchanges accurately using POS
- Receive, unpack and merchandise stock to brand presentation standards
- Keep the store clean, safe and well-presented for customers
- Work flexibly across departments depending on where the team needs help

## Requirements

- A positive, can-do attitude and genuine enjoyment of helping customers
- Comfortable learning new POS/register systems
- Reliable, punctual and a strong team player
- Available for a mix of shifts, including evenings, weekends and public holidays
- Able to stand, move and handle stock for extended periods during shifts

## Nice to have

- Previous retail or customer service experience (great, but not essential — full
  training is provided)
- Confidence assisting customers from a range of language backgrounds
- Own transport, given the store's shopping-centre location
