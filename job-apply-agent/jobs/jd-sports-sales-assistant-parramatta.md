## About the role

JD Sports Parramatta is looking for casual Sales Assistants who are passionate about
sneakers, sportswear and customer service, and who enjoy working in a fast-paced,
high-energy store environment.

## Responsibilities

- Deliver friendly, knowledgeable service to customers on the shop floor
- Operate the POS/register and handle sales, exchanges and returns
- Stock and present footwear and apparel to brand visual-merchandising standards
- Help keep the stockroom organised and the shop floor presentable
- Work as part of a close-knit team, especially during weekends and release days

## Requirements

- A confident, friendly attitude and genuine enjoyment of customer service
- Comfortable learning POS/register systems (full training provided)
- Reliable, punctual, and able to keep pace in a busy retail environment
- Available for flexible shifts — including evenings, weekends and public holidays —
  around 4 days a week
- Comfortable standing, moving and handling stock for extended periods

## Nice to have

- An interest in sneaker culture, sportswear or streetwear
- Previous retail or customer-facing experience (training provided either way)
- Ability to communicate with customers from diverse backgrounds
