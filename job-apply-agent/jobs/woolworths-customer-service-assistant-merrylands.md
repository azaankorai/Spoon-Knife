## About the role

Our Merrylands store is looking for casual Customer Service Assistants to join the
team. You'll be on hand to help customers find what they need, move through checkouts
quickly, and keep the store running smoothly — a great first step into retail.

## Responsibilities

- Greet and assist customers across checkouts, self-serve, and the shop floor
- Answer customer questions and help resolve simple issues on the spot
- Replenish stock, check use-by dates and keep shelves and displays presentable
- Follow safety, hygiene and store-presentation procedures
- Support team members during busy periods, including weekends and specials launches

## Requirements

- Friendly, approachable manner and a willingness to go the extra mile for customers
- Good communication skills and comfort working in a team
- Punctual, dependable, and able to follow instructions from supervisors
- Flexible availability, including evenings, weekends and public holidays
- Confidence picking up new systems such as POS/registers (full training provided)

## Nice to have

- Other languages spoken, given Merrylands' diverse local community
- Previous customer-facing experience (not required — full training given)
- A driver's licence and reliable transport
