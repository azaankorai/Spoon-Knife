## About the role

Our Merrylands store is looking for casual Store Assistants who thrive in a busy,
fast-paced environment and take pride in great customer service. ALDI Store
Assistants do a bit of everything — checkouts, shelf-stocking, and keeping the store
looking its best.

## Responsibilities

- Operate registers efficiently and provide friendly, attentive customer service
- Stock shelves quickly and accurately, rotating stock and checking dates
- Keep the store clean, tidy and well-presented throughout shifts
- Work at a steady pace as part of a small, close-knit team
- Follow safety and store procedures at all times

## Requirements

- A positive attitude, energy and willingness to keep up with a fast pace
- Strong teamwork and communication skills
- Reliable and punctual, with the flexibility to work a varied casual roster
- Available across weekdays, evenings, weekends and public holidays
- Comfortable with repetitive movement and handling stock during shifts

## Nice to have

- Previous experience in a fast-paced retail or hospitality role
- Other languages spoken, useful for serving the local community
- A driver's licence and own transport
