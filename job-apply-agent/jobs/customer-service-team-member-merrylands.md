# Customer Service Team Member — Merrylands

**Company:** Greenway Supermarket
**Location:** Merrylands, NSW (on-site)
**Posted:** 2026-05-28
**Link:** https://example.com/jobs/greenway-supermarket-customer-service

## About the role

Greenway Supermarket is hiring casual Customer Service Team Members for our Merrylands
store. This is a great entry-level role for someone who's reliable, friendly and happy
to pitch in across checkouts, the service desk and the shop floor.

## Responsibilities

- Operate registers and assist customers with purchases, queries and complaints
- Help at the service desk with returns, refunds and general enquiries
- Stock shelves, rotate products and keep aisles tidy and safe
- Support the team during busy periods (weekends, evenings, public holidays)
- Follow store procedures for safety, hygiene and presentation

## Requirements

- Strong communication skills and a positive, helpful attitude
- Comfortable working as part of a team in a fast-paced environment
- Punctual and dependable — able to commit to a regular casual roster
- Available across at least 4 days a week, including weekends
- Basic numeracy for handling cash and POS transactions

## Nice to have

- Other languages spoken at home (we serve a very diverse local community)
- A current driver's licence
- Willingness to obtain a Responsible Service of Alcohol (RSA) or Food Safety
  certificate if required for the role
