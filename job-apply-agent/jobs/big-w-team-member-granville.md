## About the role

Big W Granville is looking for casual Team Members to help deliver friendly service
and a great shopping experience across the store — from registers and the service
desk to stocking shelves on the floor.

## Responsibilities

- Serve customers at registers and self-serve checkouts, and assist at the service desk
- Help customers find products and answer general questions on the shop floor
- Receive, unpack and merchandise stock to presentation standards
- Keep aisles, displays and work areas clean, tidy and safe
- Support the team during peak periods (weekends, sales events, public holidays)

## Requirements

- A friendly, helpful approach and genuine enjoyment of working with people
- Comfortable learning POS/register systems (full training provided)
- Reliable, punctual, and a good team player
- Available across a varied roster, including evenings, weekends and public holidays —
  around 4 days a week
- Comfortable with light stock handling and being on your feet during shifts

## Nice to have

- Other languages spoken — useful for Big W's diverse customer base in Western Sydney
- A driver's licence and own transport
- Previous customer service or retail experience (not essential — full training given)
