# Retail Sales Assistant — Westfield Parramatta

**Company:** Northside Fashion Co.
**Location:** Westfield Parramatta, NSW (in-store)
**Posted:** 2026-06-02
**Link:** https://example.com/jobs/northside-fashion-retail-assistant

## About the role

Northside Fashion Co. is looking for a casual Retail Sales Assistant to join our
Parramatta store. You'll be the first friendly face customers see — helping them find
what they need, keeping the floor looking great, and pitching in wherever the team
needs a hand during busy trading periods.

## Responsibilities

- Greet and assist customers on the shop floor and at the register (POS)
- Process sales, returns and exchanges accurately
- Receive stock, restock shelves and keep displays neat and presentable
- Work as part of a small team to keep the store running smoothly during peak times
- Help maintain a clean, safe and welcoming store environment

## Requirements

- Friendly, can-do attitude and genuine enjoyment of helping people
- Comfortable learning POS / register systems
- Reliable, punctual, and able to work as part of a team
- Available for flexible shifts including evenings, weekends and public holidays
- Own transport preferred (store is in a shopping centre with limited public transport
  after hours)

## Nice to have

- Previous customer service or retail experience (not essential — full training given)
- Confidence speaking with a wide range of customers, including non-English speakers
