## About the role

Rebel Parramatta is after enthusiastic casual Team Members to help customers find the
right footwear, apparel and equipment for their sport or fitness goals, while keeping
the store running smoothly on the floor and at the registers.

## Responsibilities

- Greet customers and help them find products that suit their needs
- Operate the POS/register and process sales, exchanges and returns
- Stock, fold and merchandise apparel, footwear and equipment to brand standards
- Keep the shop floor, fitting areas and stockroom tidy and well-presented
- Support the team during busy periods (weekends, sales, public holidays)

## Requirements

- A friendly, energetic manner and genuine interest in helping customers
- Comfortable learning POS/register systems and product ranges (training provided)
- Reliable, punctual, and a strong team player in a fast-paced retail environment
- Available for flexible shifts — including evenings, weekends and public holidays —
  around 4 days a week
- Comfortable with light stock handling and being active on the shop floor

## Nice to have

- An interest in sport, fitness or an active lifestyle
- Previous retail or customer service experience (great, but not essential)
- Confidence assisting customers from a range of language backgrounds
